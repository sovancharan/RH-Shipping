International Ocean Freight

We have one of the most reliable global networks of ocean transportation service providers for imports and exports to and from anywhere in the world.

- Services tailored to each kind of cargo, client and buyer-seller arrangement.

- Customs brokerage services at origin and destination.

- Web tracking and personalized 24/7 service.

- FCL, LCL, break bulk, RoRo, chartering and refrigerated cargo.

- Special Projects and vessel chartering.

FCL Shipping

We transport your merchandise according to its peculiarities, employing the full container type which best suits each product.

LCL Shipping

We provide a consolidated Ocean Freight solution to any destination worldwide, without the need to wait for shipment or arrival of a whole container.

Break Break Bulk & RoRo

We transport break bulk cargo which is not possible to transport in a container, and RoRo cargo which needs specialized rolling equipment to be unloaded.
